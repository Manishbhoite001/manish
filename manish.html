Our Story

Rooted in Nashik's Farming Heart

Incorporated on 12 February 2015, Nutrifert Agri Inputs Private Limited is headquartered in Panchavati, Nashik, Maharashtra. We are engaged in agricultural and animal husbandry service activities, delivering quality agri-inputs to farmers across the region with a commitment to soil health and sustainable productivity.

What We Offer

Complete Farming Solutions

From soil testing to doorstep delivery — we support farmers every step of the way.

🌱

Seed Supply

बियाणे पुरवठा

Certified, high-germination seeds for all major Maharashtra crops — wheat, onion, cotton, soybean, and vegetables. Lab-tested for purity and seed vigour.

🧪

Soil & Water Testing

माती व पाणी परीक्षण

On-site soil health analysis with nutrient recommendations. Water quality testing for irrigation suitability. Reports delivered in Marathi for easy understanding.

🌿

Crop Nutrition Planning

पीक पोषण नियोजन

Expert agronomists design customised fertilizer and micronutrient schedules based on crop type, soil condition, and seasonal calendar for maximum yield.

🚚

Doorstep Delivery

दारापर्यंत डिलिव्हरी

Fast, reliable delivery of agri-inputs directly to the farm gate across Nashik, Ahmednagar, Pune, and neighbouring districts. Bulk orders welcome.

📱

Farmer Advisory

शेतकरी सल्ला सेवा

Phone and WhatsApp-based advisory service in Marathi. Get real-time guidance on pest management, irrigation, and crop nutrition from our experienced team.

💰

Govt. Scheme Support

सरकारी योजना सहाय्य

Helping farmers access PM-KISAN, Kisan Credit Cards, crop insurance (PMFBY), and subsidy schemes with dedicated documentation and application support.
